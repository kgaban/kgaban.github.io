2/2/18

This is the first post on my *new* blog! It's been a while since I've done this... I used to have a bunch of blog posts on my old website, but I didn't feel like paying for the domain name this year haha. I actually like the setup of GitHub Pages though, so hopefully I can get back to posting regularly. Where do we start? It's 11:10pm on Friday night and all I can think about is how little time I have before graduation. Sure there's still a lot of work to do in between, but these next few months are going to go quick. The theme of this post is going to be about motivation.

Wow, how original am I right? No seriously, sometimes you just need to... do something. It's so easy to just waste time especially with the Internet at our disposal. It's a double-edged sword though. We live in the age of infinite knowledge. That means you can learn anything you want, whenever you want. You just have to be motivated. Sometime last year I really wanted to learn how to make music. Now I don't mean playing instruments (which I definitely do want to learn) but I wanted to learn how to make music on a Computer. I am a CS major after all. I was motivated after I heard that the song Rockstar by Post Malone had the beat made by a college student. That song was #1 for how many weeks? It's crazy because well, anyone can do it (infinite knowledge remember?) So I figured, why not me? I downloaded FL Studio 12 and got to it. I sucked bad when I started. Arguably I still do haha. But it's fun and it's interesting to see how when you break down certain songs you can see all the different pieces working together. Whenever I hear new songs, I can't help but think about how I would create it on my computer. Note to self: Seriously, invest in the MIDI keyboard you've been looking at for months.
